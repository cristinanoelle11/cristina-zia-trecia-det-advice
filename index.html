About Kode With Klossy

Kode With Klossy is a 2-week summer camp, created Karlie Kloss. These young girls will learn coding in developing their own website. the preview page.

This program travels around the country to major cities. No experience in the coding field is required for this course. Only dedication and hardwork!

Young women can range between the age of 13-18 years of age! They will be able to work together and independtly to grow leanly and socially

Frequently asked Questions

Do I need to bring food anything?

No, everything that you will need for the camp is provided from lunch and snacks to a laptop.

Do I need any experience to apply?

To apply no experience is required or needed. However, previous experience is also welcomed!

How long is the camp?

The camp will take place over a 2 week span, Monday through Friday from 9-5.
